What to Expect

First Session: We'll start with a comprehensive assessment of your dog's current behavior, your goals, and create a customized training plan tailored specifically to your needs.

Training Philosophy: We use positive reinforcement methods that are effective, humane, and build a trusting relationship between you and your dog. No harsh corrections, no fear-based tactics—just proven techniques that work.

Location: Training sessions can take place at your home, a local park, or another agreed-upon location that works best for your training goals.

Package Options

Save when you commit to multiple sessions! Contact us to discuss package pricing options:

  • 4-Session Package: Perfect for addressing specific behaviors
  • 8-Session Package: Comprehensive training for major goals
  • 12-Session Package: Complete behavior transformation

All packages must be used within 6 months of purchase and include email support between sessions.
