INTUITIVE TRAINING FROM THE HEART

WELCOME TO CANINEKIND!

Our mission is to help dog owners from all walks of life form a relationship with their dog that they can be proud of. We blend an intuitive understanding of canine behavior and motivation with a full spectrum approach to training. At Caninekind, we work with the individual dog in front of us. We believe that there are many paths to Rome. Our goal is to provide you with the tools, honest feedback and education needed to address your goals for your dog long after we hand over the leash.

WHY CANINEKIND?

As the old saying goes...the proof is in the pudding!

We pride ourselves in the precision and energy of our training and the way our dogs look when working with us and for us. Read about our background, training history and watch videos of our advanced dog training skills on our "About us" page and "Videos" page. Marisa is an AKC Canine Good Citizen Evaluator and member of the International Association for Canine Professionals.

Training doesn't have to be unaffordable. We are transparent about our pricing from the get-go and understand that paying for professional dog training is a huge commitment! We offer affordable pricing and competitive training and dog walking packages designed to achieve long term results that are also affordable. Check out our "Training Services" page for more information on our training services and packages.

A NUANCED APPROACH TO DOG TRAINING

Read our "FAQs" page to understand how we approach our training philosophy. We don't simply believe in prescribing to one camp of training or another and never apply a one size fits all approach. We believe that different approaches and applications of behavioral psychology benefit each dog and owner differently.

The very first step in our process involves getting to know just about everything about your dog! Being prepared is the first step to success. We use as much information as possible to determine a training protocol that works for you, your dog and your lifestyle.

LETS TRAIN!

We will come to you!

We offer a variety of training services and assist in achieving skills like loose leash walking, house manners, door and car manners, off leash reliability, crate manners, settling/place training, trick training and overall engagement with you and your dog's desire to work for you in a consistent and fair way. On a case by case basis, we also offer assistance in evaluating behavioral issues such as reactivity, separation anxiety resource guarding and similar issues.

If you are a new client, to determine if CanineKind is a good fit for you and your dog's training goals - fill out our "Intake Form" before scheduling your free 15 minute phone consultation! This helps us help you immensely!
