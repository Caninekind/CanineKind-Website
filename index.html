Welcome to CanineKind — our mission is to help dog owners from all walks of life form a relationship with their dog that they can be proud of. We blend an intuitive understanding of canine behavior and motivation with a full spectrum approach to training. At CanineKind we work with the individual dog in front of us. We believe there are many paths to Rome. Our goal is to provide you with the tools, honest feedback and education needed to address your goals for your dog long after we hand over the leash.
