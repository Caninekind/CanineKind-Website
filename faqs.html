FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What training methods do you use?

I believe every dog and family is unique, and therefore CanineKind does not fit into a single training label when it comes to our interactions with each client. The CanineKind approach is about finding what works best for your dog, your lifestyle and your goals. I tailor each training plan based on three key factors:

1. your available and desired time commitment;
2. the level and intensity of intervention your dog needs, and
3. their individual personality and learning style.

Each approach is tailored towards applying the least intrusive method in achieving your desired results with your dog. We are very equipped to work across a range of training techniques— ranging from strictly positive reinforcement to balanced approaches using tools like tens unit e-collars, prong collars, and slip leads when appropriate.

My priority is honest, effective training that gets results that work for you. We take the time to have an open conversation during our phone consultation and first lesson to ensure that you have a realistic expectation of what approaches will be recommended for your dog. If you have already come from other training situations that were unable to provide long lasting results for you and your dog, part of my job is identifying the way previous approaches went wrong and all the options you have to address issues with your dog.

What makes your dog walking sessions different than a typical dog walker?

We are extremely excited to offer our loose leash walking outpatient program. These sessions are designed to teach your dog reliable loose leash walking skills while fitting seamlessly into your schedule.

This isn't just a dog walk—it's active training. Unlike a typical dog walking service where your dog gets exercise but the pulling and leash manners stay the same (or get worse), these sessions focus on actually teaching your dog how to walk politely on leash. Every walk is a structured training opportunity using proven methods that create lasting behavioral change.

Choose the format that works best for you:

Private Training Sessions – Work one-on-one with our trainer to learn how to teach your dog proper leash manners. We'll walk together while I coach you through the techniques, so you can see exactly what to do and practice in real-time.

Remote Training Walks – We will pick up your dog while you're at work or busy and work on their leash skills during our session. You'll receive clear homework and guidance so you can reinforce what they're learning, even if you weren't there for the training. Your dog gets exercise and education, and you get a dog who actually walks better instead of one who's just tired.

Both options follow a systematic protocol that breaks down loose leash walking into manageable steps. You'll get straightforward instructions and a progressive plan that anyone can follow—no prior dog training experience needed. Whether you're learning alongside your dog or building on the work I do with them, you'll have the tools to create lasting results.

What is your cancellation and refund policy for training lessons?

CANCELLATION & REFUND POLICY

We understand that schedules change. This policy helps protect both your time and ours by setting clear expectations for cancellations, rescheduling, and refunds. All appointments are scheduled through Square Appointments.

24-Hour Notice Required

• Free cancellation or rescheduling is available up to 24 hours before your scheduled appointment time
• You may cancel or reschedule yourself online through Square Appointments up until the 24-hour cutoff
• Simply use the link in your confirmation email or text message

Within 24 Hours (Late Cancellation)

If you cancel or reschedule with less than 24 hours notice:
• You will be charged 50% of the service fee OR $30 flat fee (whichever applies to your service)
• This fee will be automatically charged to the card on file with your permission
• At the trainer's discretion, this fee may be waived for emergencies (see Emergency Exceptions below)

No-Show Policy

If you miss your appointment without any notice:
• You will be charged the full service fee (100%)
• This will be automatically charged to the card on file
• Future bookings may require prepayment

Same-Day Emergency Cancellations

We understand that true emergencies happen. Same-day cancellations may be waived at the trainer's discretion for:
• Medical emergencies (human or dog)
• Family emergencies
• Severe weather that makes travel unsafe
• Other unavoidable circumstances

To request a waiver: Contact us immediately by email at caninekindtraining@gmail.com
Do not rely solely on the automated cancellation system for emergencies.

PACKAGE & MULTI-SESSION DEALS

Package Cancellation
If you purchase a package (e.g., 5-session bundle) and need to cancel:
  ◦ Before first session: Full refund available
  ◦ After first session has occurred: No refund on unused sessions, but sessions do not expire and can be rescheduled
  ◦ Standard 24-hour cancellation policy applies to each individual session

Package Expiration
• Multi-session packages are valid for 1 year from date of purchase
• Extensions may be granted for medical or family emergencies
• Unused sessions after expiration are forfeited

Mutual Satisfaction Guarantee

If either you or the trainer feels that the training relationship isn't working out, we offer the following mid-package refund option:

After completing at least half of the total sessions of a multi-session package, either party may request to end the training arrangement. In this case:
• You will receive a refund for all unused sessions
• This option is NOT available for single-session cancellations (standard cancellation policy applies)

TRAINER-INITIATED CANCELLATIONS

If We Cancel
Sometimes we need to cancel due to illness, emergency, or other unforeseen circumstances. If this happens:
• You will be notified as soon as possible
• You will receive a full refund OR
• We will offer to reschedule at your convenience
• No cancellation fees will apply to you

Weather Cancellations
• If severe weather makes training unsafe or travel impossible, we will cancel and offer to reschedule at no charge
• For light rain or snow, training typically proceeds
